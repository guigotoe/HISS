CC   -!- FUNCTION: Protein phosphatase which antagonizes mitotic cyclin-
CC       dependent kinase nimX, the inactivation of which is essential for
CC       exit from mitosis. To access its substrates, is released from
CC       nucleolar sequestration during mitosis. Plays an essential in
CC       coordinating the nuclear division cycle with cytokinesis through
CC       the cytokinesis checkpoint. Involved in chromosome segregation,
CC       where it is required for meiosis I spindle dissambly as well as
CC       for establishing two consecutive chromosome segregation phases (By
CC       similarity). Required for the transcription of the two major
CC       endoglucanase genes eglA and eglB and growth on synthetic
CC       cellulose as the sole carbon source. {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23800192}.
