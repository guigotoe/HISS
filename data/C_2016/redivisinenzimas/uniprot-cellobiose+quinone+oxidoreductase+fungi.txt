CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the single-oxidation or sequential double
CC       oxidation reaction of carbohydrates primarily at carbon-2 and/or
CC       carbon-3 with the concomitant reduction of the flavin. The enzyme
CC       exhibits a broad sugar substrate specificity, oxidizing different
CC       aldopyranoses to the corresponding C-1, C-2, C-3 or C-1,2, C-2,3
CC       and C-3,4 (di)dehydro sugars with substrate-specific
CC       regioselectivity. Accepts only a narrow range of electron
CC       acceptors such as substituted benzoquinones and complexed metal
CC       ions and reacts extremely slowly with O(2) as acceptor. May play a
CC       role in the natural recycling of plant matter by oxidizing all

CC       compounds or reactive radical species generated during lignin
